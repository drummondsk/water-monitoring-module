High Initial Acquisition Costs


  • Water monitoring via precision agriculture requires a substantial initial capital investment to acquire specialized sensing and data management equipment, maintenance, and setup.


  • To begin with, the adoption of precision agriculture technology requires high upfront acquisition costs.

  • Due to these economic constraints, widespread adoption of this technology may face challenges as small-scale farmers lack the capital for such an investment (AJ).
