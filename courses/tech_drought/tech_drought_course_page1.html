The Role of Technology in Drought Mitigation and Management


  • Ongoing research efforts have led to a proposal of smart irrigation as a mitigation solution.

  • Widespread adoption of this technology in agriculture can allow for more educated and accurate decision-making on irrigation timing, water quantity, and quality based on collected data (Fathy and Ali 3)

  • Researchers believe that precision agriculture can mitigate groundwater depletion by implementing remote monitoring for variable-rate irrigation systems (West and Kovacs 13).

  • Smart irrigation can be defined as a precision agriculture technique to control water consumption in agriculture by remotely monitoring soil parameters.
