Benefits of Precision Agriculture


  • Technologies such as global positioning systems, remote sensing, and geographic information systems to aid in more data-driven and responsible decision making about crop and soil management

  • Global incorporation is crucial as such a system can provide real time feedback on environmental variables (Mohd Kassim et al. 1).

  • As proper management and allocation of our water resources is key, precision agriculture provides a unique means by which farmers can become more conscious of their excessive usage of irrigation water.

  • By utilizing these advanced technologies, farmers can optimize their irrigation practices, reduce water waste, and ultimately increase crop yields.

  • Overall, precision agriculture has emerged as a viable solution to manage water resources and ensure adequate water availability to provide for the expanding population needs.
